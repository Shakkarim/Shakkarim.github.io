Welcome!

Hi there! I am an atmospheric scientist with strong experience in running, analyzing, and validating numerical simulations using NWP models to investigate multiscale atmospheric processes, including convection, boundary layer turbulence, urban-atmosphere interaction, wildfire-conducive environments, orographic impacts, and tropical wave dynamics, etc.

My current work includes:

  • Doppler radar wind retrieval and radar simulator development
  • Satellite-assimilated severe weather forecasting
  • Tropical cyclone and urban interaction
  • Large-eddy simulation of convection and turbulence

To better understand complex, multi-scale weather processes and support real-time forecasting applications, I use a variety of datasets and methods including high-resolution numerical models, atmospheric observations, radar and satellite remote sensing.
