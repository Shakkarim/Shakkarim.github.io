Welcome!

Hi there! I am an atmospheric scientist working on radar meteorology, severe weather dynamics, large-eddy simulation, numerical weather prediction, and remote sensing applications. My research focuses on understanding high-impact atmospheric processes across multiple scales.

My current research includes:

  • Doppler radar wind retrieval and radar simulator development
  • Satellite-assimilated severe weather forecasting
  • Tropical cyclone and urban interaction
  • Large-eddy simulation of convection and turbulence

To better understand complex, multi-scale atmospheric processes, I use a variety of datasets and methods including radar observations, satellite retrievals, high-resolution numerical models.
