Understanding severe weather and environmental extremes across scales

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Hampton University’s Severe Weather Research Center. My work integrates multi-Doppler radar retrievals, large-eddy simulations, numerical weather prediction, and remote sensing to investigate severe convection, turbulence, urban-weather interactions, wildfire environments, and tropical cyclone processes.
